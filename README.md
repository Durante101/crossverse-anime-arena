# Crossverse Anime Arena

## 🎮 Overview
A Java-based text RPG where players choose an anime origin and battle enemies from other universes using turn-based combat, AI-driven enemies, and a progression system.

The game focuses on turn-based combat, progression, and strategic decision-making through skills, items, and resource management.

---

## 🧠 Core Concept
- Players choose an anime origin based on color
- You do NOT fight characters from your chosen anime
- You battle enemies from other anime universes
- Each level contains 5 stages with different enemies
- Enemies have randomized behavior (attack, defend, wait, recover, special)

---

## ⚔️ Gameplay Features

### 🎨 Anime Selection System
- Choose a color → determines your anime origin
- Each origin may influence stats and abilities (future feature)

### 🧍 Player System
- Name, stats, and progression
- Limited inventory capacity
- Earn money through battles

### 👾 Enemy System
- Enemies from different anime universes
- Unique abilities and behaviors
- AI-driven decision making

### ⚔️ Battle System
- Turn-based combat
- Player chooses actions (attack, defend, skill, item)
- Enemy actions are randomized with logic

### 🧠 AI System
- Enemies can:
    - Attack
    - Defend
    - Wait
    - Use abilities
    - Heal

### 💰 Economy System
- Earn money after battles
- Spend money in shop

### 🧪 Items & Skills
- Unlock enemy-based skills after defeating them
- Buy and equip skills
- Use items during battle

---

## 📈 Progression System
- Levels contain 5 stages
- Completing a level unlocks new enemies and rewards
- Difficulty increases over time
